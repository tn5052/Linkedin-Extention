Prompt Engineering Guide

Craft effective prompts for better AI-generated comments on LinkedIn. Use variables to incorporate dynamic content.

These variables will be automatically replaced with post content, image descriptions, and your business information.

Business Context

Profile Info

Provide information about your business that helps the AI generate relevant and personalized comments. This context will be included in all comment prompts using the ${businessContext} variable.

Best Practices for Business Context:

  • Be specific about your industry, products/services, and target audience
  • Include your expertise and areas of professional interest
  • Mention tone preferences (professional, friendly, technical, etc.)
  • Specify engagement goals (e.g., building relationships, showcasing expertise)
  • Avoid overly promotional language that could make comments seem like spam
Example Business Context:
"I'm a cybersecurity consultant specializing in data protection for financial institutions. My expertise includes cloud security architecture, compliance (GDPR, PCI-DSS), and security awareness training. I aim to build relationships with financial technology professionals by sharing valuable insights rather than selling services directly. My tone should be professional but approachable, technically accurate but not overly complex."

Vision Analysis Prompt

Mistral Vision

This prompt instructs the AI how to analyze and describe images in LinkedIn posts. Focus on professional visual elements that provide context for engagement.

Tips for Vision Analysis Prompts:

  • Request objective descriptions of visual elements relevant to professional networking
  • Ask the AI to identify business-related context in images (charts, presentations, events)
  • Encourage concise descriptions that highlight key points rather than exhaustive details
  • Focus on professional relevance rather than aesthetic qualities

Comment Generation Prompt

Gemini

This prompt guides the AI in generating relevant, engaging comments for LinkedIn posts based on the post content and your business context.

Tips for Comment Generation Prompts:

  • Structure your prompt with clear sections (role, context, instructions)
  • Provide explicit instructions on tone, length, and style
  • Include negative guidance (what to avoid) to prevent generic comments
  • Request specific outputs like questions, insights, or connections to the topic
  • Specify the format for the AI's response to ensure consistency
